Pure Mochi Magic

Crispy browned caramelized edges meet a bouncy, melt-in-your-mouth interior.

🍡

The Legendary Chewy Bounce

Made using premium sweet glutinous rice flour (Mochiko), our dough undergoes a slow-rise temperature bake to lock in that incredibly soft, elastic, and elastic pull that defines perfect Hawaiian-style mochi.

🧈

Rich, Buttery Slow-Baked Core

We fold blocks of real organic sweet cream butter and rich coconut cream into the recipe. This creates an unbelievably aromatic and decadent custard core that stays fresh, buttery, and incredibly moist for days.

🥥

The Crunchy Coconut Surprise

The crown jewel. Right before finishing, we dust the caramelized golden top with hand-shaved white coconut shreds. They roast to a crisp, providing a brilliant, unexpected crunch that contrasts beautifully with the soft cake below.

The Artisan's Touch
"I wanted to craft a pastry that feels like a warm hug. By combining the slow, gentle methods of traditional Japanese mochi with the sunny, decadent, buttery flavors of Hawaiian butter mochi, I found a texture that is truly heavenly. The golden coconut shreds on top are my personal signature—giving you a delightful, crispy crunch when you least expect it."
— Takako Founder & Head Baker
